## Bugs Found

### BUG 1 (CRITICAL): Combat interrupt causes "unknown action" error
- **Repro**: NPC is gathering in a forest with enemies. Enemy attacks reduce HP below 50.
- **What happens**: tick.go combat interrupt sets `BusyUntilTick = e.TickCount()` and `PendingActionID = ""`. Then `processCompletionsAndInterrupts` sees `BusyUntilTick <= currentTick` and executes the empty action string, producing "Stood around, confused (unknown action: )."
- **Impact**: Wasted action tick, confusing memory entry, NPC loses a turn.
- **Observed**: 2 times during this session.
- **Fix**: Set `BusyUntilTick = 0` instead of `e.TickCount()` in the combat interrupt code.

### BUG 2 (GAMEPLAY): HP < 15 interrupt fires without enemies present
- **Repro**: NPC flees to a safe location (no enemies) at low HP. Any action committed gets interrupted because HP < 15.
- **What happens**: turns.go interrupt loop checks `n.HP < 15` unconditionally (no enemy check). At HP 12, NPC at Garden Well (safe) couldn't eat, drink, reflect, or tell stories - every action was immediately interrupted and resumed.
- **Impact**: NPCs are trapped in an unusable state at low HP until natural regen brings them above 15. This takes 15+ ticks of doing nothing.
- **Fix**: Change `if n.HP < 15` to `if len(enemiesHere) > 0 && n.HP < 15`.

- No way to self-heal without medicine items or a healer NPC
- Homeless NPCs have no affordable rest option (rough sleep causes HP/happiness loss)
- Herbs exist as medicine items but only "heal" action uses them (requires being in inventory)

- Weather system exists (clear/cloudy/rain/storm) but has zero gameplay impact
- No actions are modified by weather
- Stargaze doesn't check weather (could stargaze in a storm)

## New Features Implemented

### Weather Effects on Actions
- Forage: clear +10% yield, rain -15% yield +3 fatigue, storm -30% yield +6 fatigue
- Hunt: weather modifies effective skill for hunt success chance
- Fish: rain +20% catch (fish surface!), storm -25% catch +5 fatigue
- Gather firewood: storms knock extra branches loose (+1 bonus log)
- Cook over fire: blocked during storms (can't light fire in storm)
- Stargaze: blocked during rain/storms (can't see stars)
- Travel: rain +30% fatigue, storm +60% fatigue

### Lean-to Shelter (craft_shelter + camp_rest)
- craft_shelter: 2 logs + 2 thatch -> lean-to shelter item
- camp_rest: sleep outdoors with shelter, much better than rough sleeping
  - Clear: -35 fatigue, -5 stress, +4 HP
  - Cloudy: -30 fatigue, -3 stress, +3 HP
  - Rain: -25 fatigue, -3 stress, +3 HP (shelter keeps you dry)
  - Storm: -20 fatigue, +2 stress, +1 HP (shelter shakes but holds)
- Fills the death spiral gap for homeless NPCs

### Apply Herbs (self-heal)
- Use 1 herbs to heal 8-15 HP
- Available at HP < 70, no healer needed
- Gains herbalism skill
- Fills solo healing gap
