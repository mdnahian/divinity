## Phase 1: Initial Observations

### Bugs Found & Fixed
1. **DUPLICATE MEDITATE ACTION** (CRITICAL): wellbeing.go had TWO meditate definitions with the same ID "meditate" but different stats/labels. FindAction returned first match, making the richer second definition (+spiritual insight) dead code. list_actions showed both, confusing the UI. **FIX**: Removed the weaker first definition, kept the richer one, and merged the social need recovery from the first into the second.

2. **Flee enemy name bug** (MEDIUM): `flee_area.Execute` checked `w.EnemiesAtLocation(n.LocationID)` but by the time Execute runs, the NPC has already been moved to the safe destination. Always showed "Fled from danger" instead of naming the actual enemy. **FIX**: Use `n.PreviousLocationID` (set before travel by engine) to check enemies at the original location.

3. **Scavenge equal-value tie-breaking** (LOW): When items had equal GetPrice*qty (e.g., bread x2 and logs x2 both = 4), the stable sort preserved insertion order, causing bread to always be picked before materials. Market had 37 bread stacks hiding 2 log stacks. **FIX**: Added category-based tie-breaker that prioritizes materials > medicine > trade goods > curiosities > food.

### Features Confirmed Working (from merged PR#14)
- **scavenge sort**: Iron ore (higher value) picked before bread. Working.
- **meditate**: Works (-5 stress, -10 fatigue, +3 happiness, -8 social). Second definition was unreachable (now removed).
- **stargaze**: Works at night (22:00+) at outdoor locations. Tested at Holy Stone Shrine.
- **tend_shrine**: +2 reputation confirmed. Offering system works with curiosity items.
- **reflect**: Works when SocialNeed > 30. -15 social need recovery confirmed.
- **auto-interrupt**: Triggered at HP < 50 during forage when wolves attacked. Freed NPC to flee next tick.
- **smart flee**: Picked nearby safe locations (Eldrin Thorne's Home, Garden Well) instead of random distant ones.
- **sleep text**: Not directly tested (Cassia always had inn access).

### Design Issues Found
- Sleep consumes 50+ game ticks (240 game minutes = massive real-time wait in playtest)
- Wolves attack at farms (Oakhaven Farm) making farming dangerous with no advance warning
- Zero NPCs encountered in 21+ committed ticks across multiple locations (solo world)
- Stress reaches 100 easily after 3 wolf attacks with no fast recovery mechanism
- No way to gather logs without an iron axe (now fixed with gather_firewood)

## Phase 4: Bugs Fixed & New Features

### Bugs Fixed (3)
1. Removed duplicate meditate, merged best of both into single action
2. Fixed flee_area to show actual enemy name using PreviousLocationID
3. Fixed scavenge tie-breaking to prioritize materials over food

### New Features Implemented (5, ALL different from PRs #13-17)
1. **tell_story** (wellbeing): Solo entertainment/lore action. -6 stress, +4 happiness, -6 social need, builds storytelling skill. At skill 30+, grants wisdom. Nearby NPCs get +2 happiness. Available anywhere, no items needed.

2. **gather_firewood** (gather): Collect fallen branches from forest floor. No axe needed. Slower than chop_wood (40 min vs 45 min) but yields 1 log with no tool requirement. Fills the critical gap where logs were unobtainable for non-axe holders.

3. **cook_over_fire** (craft): Cook raw meat or fish anywhere outdoors using 1 log as fuel. Produces cooked meal (from meat) or grilled fish (from fish, new item with 30 hunger restore). No inn, no campfire system, no profession needed. Solo-friendly cooking.

4. **craft_fishing_rod** (craft): Craft fishing rod from 1 log + 1 rope. The rod is a weapon-slot item (minimal combat) that gives +15% catch chance and +1 bonus fish per catch when equipped.

5. **mend_equipment** (craft): Self-repair equipped weapon or armor using raw materials. Stone/iron ingot for weapons, leather/cloth for armor. Iron ingot restores +35 durability, stone +20, leather +30, cloth +15. Available to all NPCs.

### New Items (2)
- **grilled fish**: Food item with 30 hunger restore (between fish at 12 and cooked meal at 40)
- **fishing rod**: Weapon-slot item with +1 weapon bonus and fishing_bonus effect

### Polish
- Meditate now also reduces social need by 5 (merged from deleted duplicate)
- Meditate condition now also blocks if last action was "pray" (prevents pray/meditate spam loop)
