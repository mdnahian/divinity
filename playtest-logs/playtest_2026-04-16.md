## Bugs Found

### BUG 1 (CRITICAL): Action memories evicted by combat memories
- **Repro**: Any non-combat action adds a memory with Importance=0.1 (see `server/engine/turns.go` lines 71–76). Enemy attack memories use Importance=0.9. MaxMemories is 20, and the eviction logic in `server/memory/memory.go:187-198` removes the least-important entry when over cap.
- **Observed**: After 20+ actions including sleep, trade, tell_story, meditate, tend_shrine, scavenge, reflect, travel, eat, drink, the `recall_memories` tool returned ONLY combat memories. All routine and social memories had been pushed out by the 14 wolf-attack memories that occurred during the session.
- **Impact**: NPCs forget they slept, what they bought, who they talked to — only remembering being attacked. This breaks agent decision-making: an NPC can't use memories of "I tried foraging at Whispering Pines and got mauled" to decide to go elsewhere, because the memory is evicted the next time the wolves hit them.
- **Fix**: Per-action importance tuning (routine=0.25, economic/social/education=0.35, meaningful=0.5, combat=0.6) so routine memories survive alongside a few vivid combat ones. See `actionMemoryImportance` in `server/engine/turns.go`.

### BUG 2 (GAMEPLAY): No viable non-forest path to logs
- **Repro**: As a non-woodcutter NPC without an iron axe, the only way to get logs is `gather_firewood` (40-minute action at a forest location).
- **Observed**: Whispering Pines has 17 wolves/dire wolves. On three separate attempts (HP 100, HP 77, HP 45), the gather action was interrupted within 5–15 game minutes by wolf damage (11–23 damage per hit). No attempt succeeded.
- **Impact**: `craft_shelter` and `camp_rest` (PR #19's headline homeless-support features) are effectively unreachable for solo non-combat NPCs in worlds with the current wolf density. The feature fixes one problem but the prerequisite chain is broken.
- **Fix**: New `salvage_wood` action at docks, mills, forges, or workshops. 60–80% success per attempt, yields 1 log, 30-minute duration, non-forest locations. See `server/action/gather.go`.

## Gameplay Observations

### Forest hostility dominates solo play
Whispering Pines: 17 enemies (mostly dire wolves, str 54–68). Shadowed Woods: 12 enemies. Both within 5–20 min travel of the main market. Each dire wolf hit dealt 8–23 damage. No non-combat NPC can safely gather in forests without an escort or combat build.

### Social isolation for non-social professions
Social stat dropped from 64 → 2 within ~15 ticks of solo play. A single `tell_story` at the market (4 listeners) brought it back up to 28. This is a nice solo-friendly recovery — but the `talk` action produced no visible memory entry due to BUG 1.

### PR #14 `meditate` + `tend_shrine` work well solo
Meditate at Sanctum of the Winds restored some HP and reduced stress. tend_shrine gave +2 reputation (53→55) and +happiness. Both feel good for a shrine visit.

### PR #18 `tell_story` consistently effective
`tell_story` at Aethelgard Market found 4 listeners and boosted social from 2→28. The "+storytelling xp" flavor also took my storytelling skill from 0 to 1.

### Weather gating feels natural
Being unable to stargaze in the rain and knowing rain helps fishing both feel like the kind of natural consequence that makes the world feel alive.

### Economy: buy_supplies may silently fail
`buy_supplies` at Aethelgard Market succeeded once (got leather for 3g from Eamon Thorne) but a second attempt didn't add anything new and didn't produce a clear failure message. The nearby sellers' inventories matter, but there's no way for the player to know what the market actually has.

## New Features Implemented (Phase 4)

### 1. Action memory importance tuning (BUG 1 fix)
- `server/engine/turns.go`: added `actionMemoryImportance(actionID)` helper.
- Routine survival/gathering memories at 0.25; economic, social, education at 0.35; meaningful milestones (sleep, craft_shelter, start_business, etc.) at 0.5; NPC's own combat actions at 0.6; movement at 0.15.
- Combat attack memories (0.9 from enemy attacks) still dominate vivid, but routine memories now survive.

### 2. `salvage_wood` action (BUG 2 fix)
- `server/action/gather.go`: new action, 30 game min, 1 log per attempt.
- Available at dock (80% success), mill (75%), forge (60%), workshop (55%).
- Fatigue cost 8, gains 0.1 woodcutter skill.
- Provides a solo-safe alternative path to logs so that `craft_shelter` / `craft_fishing_rod` / cook_over_fire are actually reachable.

### 3. `bandage_wound` action (self-heal alternative)
- `server/action/survival.go`: new survival action.
- Requires cloth in inventory and HP < 75.
- Heals 5–10 HP, consumes 1 cloth.
- Complements `apply_herbs` (which requires herbs); cloth is more commonly spawned with starting NPCs and sold at markets.

### 4. `name_landmark` action (solo wellbeing)
- `server/action/survival.go`: new wellbeing action, 20 game min.
- Available at outdoor natural locations (forest, farm, dock, well, shrine, cave, desert, swamp, tundra, garden, stable).
- Effect: +5 happiness, −3 stress, +2 fatigue. Creates a high-importance (0.55) vivid (0.8) memory with a location-appropriate flavor phrase.
- Non-repeatable via LastAction check — one marking per session per location chain.
- Adds a sense of personal attachment to the geography for solo NPCs.
