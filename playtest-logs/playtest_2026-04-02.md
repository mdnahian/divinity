### Ground Items at Fog Market
- 53+ bread x2 stacks, 11 gold x15 stacks before scavenge
- Source: NPC deaths drop up to 3 inventory items; all NPCs spawn with bread x2 + gold x15
- Non-gold item decay is 3/day with 80 durability = ~27 day lifespan — too slow

### Tick Rate Issues
- Configured: 60s tick interval
- Observed: 2-10 minutes per tick, highly variable
- Cause: GOD LLM turn processing blocks the tick loop
- Impact: 24-tick playtest would take 2-10 hours instead of 24 minutes

## Bugs Found

### BUG-S1 (HIGH): Scavenge has no cap on non-gold item pickup
- File: server/action/gather.go, scavenge Execute function
- A single scavenge picked up 62 bread stacks (124 bread) from the ground
- The gold cap (maxGoldPickup = 20) works correctly, but there is no equivalent cap for food/materials
- With frequent NPC deaths dropping bread, markets accumulate 50+ bread stacks
- One scavenge makes the NPC effectively immortal with 120+ bread
- Fix: Add maxItemStacks constant (5) to limit non-gold item pickups per scavenge

## Design Issues (not fixed, suggestions only)

### SEVERE: Duplicate NPC names at extreme levels
- 13 NPCs named "Elara" at one market, 4 "Elian the Silent"
- Same issue at Rat's Nest inn: 16 NPCs, mostly Elaras
- SpawnOnDemand LLM consistently generates "Elara" as name
- Previous playtests: 6-10 duplicates; now even worse at 13

### HIGH: Scribe profession has no accessible unique actions
- Scribe primary skill is "writing" but spawn gives "scribe" skill
- copy_text requires library access (all owned = blocked)
- write_journal requires home/inn/library location AND stress>40 OR social>30
- At spawn location (market), no scribe actions are available
- Effectively a profession with zero unique gameplay

### HIGH: Massive ground item accumulation at markets
- 53+ bread stacks + 11 gold stacks at Fog Market
- NPC deaths drop bread x2 and gold x15 which pile up
- Non-gold items decay at 3/day with 80 durability = 27 days
- Creates litter and memory bloat (scavenge result text was enormous)

### MEDIUM: Tick processing far slower than configured interval
- 60s configured, 2-10 min observed
- GOD LLM calls block the tick loop synchronously
- Makes real-time playtest extremely slow

### MEDIUM: Inn table fees scale badly with NPC population
- Fee = 3 + guests gold per non-exempt action at inn
- With 16+ NPCs at Rat's Nest, fee would be 19+ gold per action
- Drains NPC gold very quickly, may contribute to death spiral
